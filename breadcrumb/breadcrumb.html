Breadcrumb

There are numerous way to markup a bread crumb in HTML. Is one better than the others?

The shortest a breadcrumb will be is 1 item (a link to home). The longest a breadcrumb will be is 3 items (a link to home, a parent category and a child category. We are not displaying the current page in the breadcrumb.

1. Ordered List

  1. Home >
  2. Debt and borrowing >
  3. Mortgage and rent problems

2. Unordered list

  • Home >
  • Debt and borrowing >
  • Mortgage and rent problems

3. Paragraph

Home > Debt and borrowing > Mortgage and rent problems

4. Is it helpful to wrap the breadcrumb in a nav element and give it a label of breadcrumb?

Home > Debt and borrowing > Mortgage and rent problems
